Data journalist interested in artificial intelligence, election politics, and following the money. I started my career as a fact-checker at Pakistan's only IFCN-signatory, then pivoted to investigative reporting — working on Suisse Secrets with an OCCRP consortium for Fact Focus. After dabbling in broadcast journalism and business reporting, I returned to investigations as a senior producer for Soch Videos. I have also contributed reporting to Drop Site News. I love working with various mediums to figure out the best way to deliver a story. I create data visualizations, film, edit, and make social videos.
