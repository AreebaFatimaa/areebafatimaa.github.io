The Commonwealth Observer Group published a report claimed that Pakistan's 2024 elections were "peaceful, efficient, and transparent." However, contrary to their statement, the elections were marred by domestic terrorism threats, political violence, suppression of candidates, and serious irregularities which resulted in the creation of two sets of vote count: the fake vote count created by the government in power, and the original vote count handed out to polling agents at the end of polling day. In this investigation I dug deeper, analyzing the several inconsistencies in COG's report. Pakistani Arms in Ukraine

For more than a year, Pakistani officials denied the presence of Pakistani arms in Ukraine. I was able to triangulate information by discovering private military contracts worth $364 million, on the U.S. FPDS which were a part of the Ukraine Security Assistance Initiative. These were meant to acquire 155mm amunition rounds which were manufactured in Pakistan and sent to Ukraine through Global Military Products and Northrop Grumman Systems. This investigation was listed in the top 10 investigations from Pakistan in 2023 by the Global Investigative Journalism Network (GIJN) For Soch Videos.

PMLN leaders have made false claims regarding internet in Pakistan

After installing an internet firewall powered by deep packet inspection, Pakistani officials claimed the web management system was not causing internet disruptions in the country. They also claimed that countries like the U.K. and the U.S. have installed similar firewalls without raising concerns. For this piece, I spoke with internet experts who confirmed these statements were false.
